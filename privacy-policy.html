Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 15, 2026

The short version: MyFaceScore analyzes your photo, gives you a score, and immediately deletes the photo. We don't store your images, we don't create accounts, and we don't track you.

1. What MyFaceScore Does

MyFaceScore is an iOS app that uses AI to analyze a facial photo and provide a first-impression score. You take or select a photo, the app sends it for AI analysis, you receive your results, and the photo is deleted. That's it.

2. What Data We Collect

We keep this minimal by design:

  • Facial photo: The photo you provide is sent to our server and forwarded to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis. The photo is deleted immediately after scoring is complete. We do not store, save, or archive your images.

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or account information
  • Device identifiers or location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Cookies (MyFaceScore is a native app, not a website)

3. How Your Data Is Used

Your photo is used for one purpose only: to generate your first-impression score via AI analysis. Once the score is returned to you, the photo is deleted from our systems. We do not use your photo for training, research, or any other purpose.

4. Third-Party Services

We use one third-party service:

  • Anthropic (Claude API): Your photo is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to perform the AI analysis. Anthropic processes the image to generate your score and does not retain the image for model training when accessed via their API. You can review Anthropic's privacy practices at anthropic.com/privacy.

We do not share your data with any other third parties. There are no advertising networks, analytics services, or data brokers involved.

5. Data Retention

Your photo is held in memory only for the brief duration of the analysis (typically a few seconds) and is deleted immediately afterward. We have no database of user images. Once your score is delivered, we retain nothing.

6. Data Security

Photos are transmitted using encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Because we do not store images or personal data, the risk of a data breach exposing your information is minimal.

7. Children's Privacy

MyFaceScore is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has used the app, please contact us and we will address any concerns, though no data would have been retained.

8. Your Rights

Because we do not store personal data or maintain user accounts, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. Your photo is already gone by the time you see your score.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

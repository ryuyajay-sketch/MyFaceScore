How It Works

MyFaceScore uses AI to analyze your portrait photo and score it on four scientifically-backed dimensions: Trustworthiness, Competence, Approachability, and Attractiveness. You get brutally honest feedback plus actionable tips to improve.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my photo stored?
No. Your photo is analyzed and deleted immediately. We never store your images.

How accurate are the scores?
Scores are based on the Todorov face perception model from Princeton. They reflect how your photo may be perceived by others — not who you are as a person.

What contexts are available?
Professional (LinkedIn, resumes), Dating (dating apps), and Social (Instagram, general social media). Each context adjusts scoring weights.

Why did my analysis fail?
Make sure your photo is a clear, front-facing portrait with good lighting and only one person visible.

Can I analyze multiple photos?
Yes — go back and upload as many photos as you like.
